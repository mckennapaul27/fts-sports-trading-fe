# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Fortis Sports Trading ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

By using our website and services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the practices described in this policy, please do not use our services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

- **Register for an account:** We collect your email address, password (which is encrypted), first name, and last name.
- **Subscribe to our services:** We collect payment information through our third-party payment processor (Stripe), including billing address and payment method details.
- **Contact us:** When you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message.
- **Update your preferences:** We collect your email notification preferences, including preferences for daily selections, results updates, monthly performance reports, system updates, billing reminders, and marketing emails.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Pages visited and time spent on pages
- Referring website addresses
- Date and time of access

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and store certain information. Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are stored on your device. We use cookies for:

- Authentication and session management
- Remembering your preferences
- Analyzing website traffic and usage patterns
- Improving website functionality

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our website.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

1. **Service Delivery:** To provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

   - Processing your registration and subscription
   - Delivering daily selections and system updates
   - Providing access to results and historical data
   - Managing your account and preferences

2. **Communication:** To communicate with you about:

   - Your account and subscription status
   - Service updates and system changes
   - Daily selections and results (based on your preferences)
   - Billing and payment information
   - Responding to your inquiries and support requests

3. **Marketing:** With your consent, to send you marketing communications about our services, promotions, and updates. You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time through your account settings or by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.

4. **Legal Compliance:** To comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. **Analytics:** To analyze usage patterns, improve our website functionality, and enhance user experience.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

- Encryption of passwords using industry-standard hashing algorithms
- Secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission
- Regular security assessments and updates
- Access controls and authentication requirements
- Secure payment processing through Stripe

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide our services to you

- Enforce our agreements

When you cancel your subscription, we will retain your account information for a reasonable period to comply with legal and accounting requirements. You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.
